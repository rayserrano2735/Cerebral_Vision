Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to Digiland SMS notifications, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Your consent to receive SMS messages
  • Timestamp of your subscription

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number solely to:

  • Send you personal notifications and alerts from your digital companion
  • Provide service-related communications

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may only be shared with:

  • Service providers necessary to deliver SMS messages (e.g., Twilio)

SMS Communications

By subscribing to SMS notifications:

  • Message frequency varies based on your companion's activity
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP
  • Text HELP for assistance

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Access the information we hold about you

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@digiland.dev
